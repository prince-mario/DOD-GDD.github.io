Game summary

DOD is a iometric, action rougle-lite dungon crawl with a Gym bro theme,

Action is a the core of this game, rooms are filled with monters for you to smash in the face

it will inclue a strength progression where you can lift more heavy objects to use in combat. The levels will be rooms filled with Gym-rats and ripped Goblins, there will be 4 floors each progressively getting harder, rooms will be hand made but the order mixes up slightly each time.

We are taking inspiration from beat em up games like castle crashers or Cult Of The Lamb.
