Backstory

Chad since a young age has always been small and scrawny. When his father left he had a hard time doing the household chores and takeing care of his sick mother. Chad being so small and scrawny couldnt find a job that he could do. Eventualy the money that his father left ran out, and he couldnt afford his mothers medicine. Chad wanted a change, to become strong, yet he had no money to pay for the expensive fees of a gym. That was until he heard of the Dun-Gym of Deadlifts.
